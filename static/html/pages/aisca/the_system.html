“Make the best out of the Information in your Data”

Take all the (enterprise) information data that you have right now, at this moment, stored:

  • On any support: document, audio (speech), video, imaging...
  • In any format: text, office documents, structured database records, email, chat, audio (speech), video...

...and turn it into valuable, connected information:

  • Use intelligent queries to:
    • Get any Information you need, and see it at once, all connected, regardless of the format2
    • Get the Information language-independent3, meaning that results in the different languages can be returned by the query
    • See all the People connected with the Information

From there:

  • Do some Fine Tuning to get the Information that is most relevant to you:
    • Get Alerts (pop-ups, e-mail, sms...) whenever new relevant and interesting Information comes in, from any desired channel4
    • Create/Join the Community around the most relevant and interesting Information for you, with details on members’ participation's, involvement, knowledge and expertise...
